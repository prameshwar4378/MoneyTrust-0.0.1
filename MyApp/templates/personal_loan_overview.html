Features of Personal Loan

What makes Personal Loan a Perfect Choice!


No Collateral or Security

No collateral or mortgage to avail a personal loan. Loan approval is based on your credit score, credit report, repayment history, and other criteria.

End-Usage-Flexible

No end-usage restrictions on a Personal Loan gives you the liberty to meet multiple purposes including medical emergencies, holidays, business, or debt consolidation.

Flexible Tenure

You may avail a loan for a flexible period ranging from 12 months and 72 months.

Minimal Documentation

Identity proof, proof of residence or address, and income proof are the only requirement for a personal loan.

Quick Disbursal

Once approved, disbursal of your personal loan happens within a few hours. If you avail a pre-approved personal loan, the disbursal would be as short as a few minutes.

Flexible Loan Amount

Factors determining your loan value are repayment history, monthly income, age, profession, employer reputation, etc.
